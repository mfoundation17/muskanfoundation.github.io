MUSKAN FOUNDATION was started on 17th May 2018. Founded by a core team lives in Bhilwara, Rajasthan. The main purpose of Foundation is to help the needy ones and to spread happiness.

MUSKAN FOUNDATION WEEK

Our Foundation was started on 17th May 2018. In 2020, our team decided to celebrate it's 2nd anniversary as
MUSKAN FOUNDATION WEEK from 11th May to 17th May every year from now by serving and
having different activities all around the district.

1 11th May Food Packet Distribution
2 12th May Fodder To Cows
